Rules

General

  1. Everyone only has one target to eliminate at any given time
  2. You can only eliminate your own target
  3. For an elimination to count
    • You must use a valid 'weapon'
    • You must be in a valid location
  4. The game continues until only one assassin remains
  5. When you are emliminated, you are out of the game (you must also click the remove button)
  6. Do not tell anyone who your target is
  7. When you eliminate your target your next target is their target
  8. Non players can only be used for information. You must not tell them about the game and they can not spy for you.
